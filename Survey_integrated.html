Please read the following before participating

Purpose: This study investigates how people make evacuation decisions in building emergency scenarios and how digital information cues affect those decisions. Findings will inform an AI-based behavioural decision-support framework for Building Performance Simulation (BPS).

What's involved: You will read 4 building evacuation scenarios (with floor plans) and answer questions. This takes approximately 15–20 minutes.

Privacy: This survey is fully anonymous. No personally identifiable information is collected. Data is used for academic research only.

Voluntary: Participation is voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without consequence.

研究说明Study Instructions
接下来您将阅读 4 个建筑疏散情景

每个情景包含：一张建筑平面图（俯视图）和文字描述，说明您所在位置、火灾状况及可用信息线索，然后是若干问题。

注意：情景为虚构，请将自己代入情景中，根据您的真实判断作答。没有绝对正确或错误的答案。

您将被随机分配到三种信息线索条件之一（A / B / C），在所有情景中保持一致。

You will read 4 building evacuation scenarios

Each scenario includes: a building floor plan (top-down view) with a written description of your location, the fire situation, and available information cues, followed by questions.

Note: Scenarios are fictional. Please put yourself in the situation and respond based on your genuine judgement. There are no right or wrong answers.

You will be randomly assigned to one of three information cue conditions (A / B / C), which remains the same across all scenarios.

平面图阅读提示：每个情景提供 3D 沉浸视角（默认）和 2D 俯视平面图，可用按钮切换。3D 视角帮助您感受实际空间和烟雾情况；2D 平面图提供完整建筑布局。蓝色圆圈 = 您的位置，红色区域 = 烟雾/火源，绿色方块 = 推荐出口。 Viewing options: Each scenario provides a 3D immersive view (default) and a 2D top-down floor plan — toggle between them using the buttons. 关于本研究的说明

感谢您完成所有情景的作答。现在向您说明本研究的完整目的：

本研究比较三种数字信息线索（A：静态EXIT标志，B：机器狗引路，C：移动端推送）对疏散决策质量的影响。您被随机分配到其中一种条件，这是实验设计的一部分。

研究成果将用于开发基于AI的建筑疏散决策支持框架，改善建筑性能模拟（BPS）中的人员行为预测精度。

About this study

Thank you for completing all scenarios. We can now share the full purpose of this study:

This study compared three types of digital information cues (A: static EXIT signs, B: robot dog guide, C: mobile push notifications) on evacuation decision quality. You were randomly assigned to one condition — this was part of the experimental design.

Findings will be used to develop an AI-based behavioural decision-support framework for Building Performance Simulation (BPS) to improve the fidelity of occupant behaviour modelling in fire evacuation simulations.
